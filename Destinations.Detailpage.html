Rishikesh

The Yoga Capital of the World. Where the holy Ganges flows and spirituality meets adventure in the foothills of the Himalayas.

The Spiritual Gateway

Nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas along the banks of the sacred Ganges River, Rishikesh is a sanctuary for seekers of peace and adrenaline alike. It gained global fame after The Beatles visited in the 60s, but its roots go back thousands of years as a hub for yoga and meditation.

Whether you are here to attend the world-famous Ganga Aarti, practice yoga in a riverside ashram, or brave the white-water rapids, Rishikesh offers a unique blend of tranquility and thrill that you won't find anywhere else.

3-Day Perfect Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival & Spiritual Vibes

    Arrive in Rishikesh. Check into your riverside camp or hotel. Evening visit to **Triveni Ghat** for the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti. Dinner at a local cafe near Lakshman Jhula.

  • Day 2: Adventure & Nature

    Early morning Yoga session. Head to Shivpuri for **River Rafting** (16km stretch). Lunch at the famous "Little Buddha Cafe". Evening walk across Ram Jhula and visit The Beatles Ashram.

  • Day 3: Sunrise & Departure

    Hike to **Kunjapuri Temple** for a breathtaking sunrise over the Himalayas. Breakfast with a view. Souvenir shopping in the local market before departure.
